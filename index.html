Preparing Your Books

  • Ensure covers and pages are clean, no severe damage.
  • Remove personal notes, bookmarks, or sticky tabs.
  • Sort by genre or reader age for quick library processing.
  • Pack in sturdy boxes or reusable totes.
  • Label contents to assist library volunteers.
